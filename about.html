Who We Are

Kainan sa Tagong, is known here at Camarines Norte that serve delicious and authentic meals, and Grill Expert that has captured the hearts and appetites of many people. It is Established in 2020, and we're aming to have grown to become one of the country's most beloved fast-food chains, known for our authentic Filipino flavors and unlimited rice offering.

Our mission is to serve delicious, high-quality grilled dishes at affordable prices while maintaining the authentic taste of traditional Filipino barbecue. We take pride in our unique marinade and grilling techniques that have made our Chicken Inasal a nationwide favorite.
